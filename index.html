Info

In Ravine, you must survive as many days as you can! Gather enough food and avoid the enemies!
- Interact with your bed to progress to the next day.
- Move around with Arrow Keys.

Tips

  • - Required food per day scales with how many days have passed.
  • - Shake the trees for more food if not enough spawned on the ground, but don't shake it too much!!
